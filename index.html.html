Hi! I'm Abdul Basit, a Software Developer and UI/UX Designer based in Pakistan, passionate about building digital products that are not just functional — but delightful to use.

I have hands-on experience spanning the full product cycle: from wireframing and high-fidelity prototyping in Figma, to developing robust cross-platform applications using React Native, Node.js, Firebase, and modern automation workflows.

I thrive at the intersection of aesthetics and engineering — I believe great software should look as good as it works. Whether it's a startup MVP or a medical assistance platform, I bring both technical precision and design thinking to every project.
